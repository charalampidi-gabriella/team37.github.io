RoomSense: Autonomous Robotic Assistant

Team Number: 37

Instructors: Sara Davis, Devrin Lee, Vinh Le

External Advisors: Christos Papachristos

Short project description: Our project is a robotic assistant that autonomously navigates and identifies objects within any room. The robot will be capable of scanning its environment, mapping the layout, and detecting items. It will be made easy to use by anyone, including those who might have trouble moving around or seeing well.
